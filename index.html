COMPUTER EDUCATION

Computers are essential part of our lives. We use them to work, learn, communicate and entertain ourselves. In today’s world, it is more important than ever for children to learn how to use computers effectively. It helps develop important skills, like problem solving skills, creativity, etc.

OUR MOTIVE

With the advancement of society the use technology is part and parcel in our daily lives. One such device is the computer, computers have become an integral part of our lives, and computer education is essential for children to succeed in today’s digital world. With out platform we intend to help those students who cannot afford to learn at home. We will try to help them as much as we can by teaching them the basics of computer education.
